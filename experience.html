My Experiences during Diploma in Office Management and Technology

Textile Show Florabesque

Textile Show Florabesque is a fashion show organized by student part 5 Diploma in Art and Design at Universiti Teknologi MARA Cawangan Kelantan Kampus Machang. In this fashion show, i contributed as a model.


Kelantan Textile Show

Kelantan Textile Show is a fashion show organized by Culture Unit Theather Club and AD Brains Club at Universiti Teknologi MARA Cawangan Kelantan Kampus Machang. In this fashion show, i contributed as a crew member and also as a model.


Modelling Show

Modelling Show is a fashion show organized by Culture Unit Theather Club at Universiti Teknologi MARA Cawangan Kelantan Kampus Machang. In this fashion show, i contributed as a model.


Red Carpet GALA Dinner

Red Carpet GALA Dinner is a dinner for student part 5 Bachelor in Office System Management and student part 3 Diploma in Office Management and Technology at Universiti Teknologi MARA Cawangan Kelantan Kampus Machang. In this dinner, i contributed as emcee.


Royal Elegant Dinner

Royal Elegant Dinner is a dinner for student who participated in Persatuan Bulan Sabit Merah at Universiti Teknologi MARA Cawangan Kelantan Kampus Machang.
